  • Select five winners from each week's list of games. If you go PERFECT (five for five picked correctly), then you will earn 7 points. Anything less than perfect, you get face value for the correct picks. (3 correct picks out of 5 games = 3 points). The picks that you submit are NOT "against the spread". just pick the winner straight up

  • There is no limit on how many times you can choose a specific team.

  • If you select a team and that game ends in a tie- it is considered a LOSS.

  • The season is 17 weeks long. Playoffs will only be used to decide any ties between people in the top 5 spots.

  • Once the spreadsheet is posted, your picks are final. No last minute changes or making adjustments because you know what other people picking. Make sure to do your picks by Saturday night (midnight).

  • If you forget to do your picks, then you get defaulted to the picks that you submitted the previous week.

  • If you go "perfect" 3 weeks in a row, that 3rd perfect will result in 8 points rather than 7 points.

  • After you've reached your 4th perfect week all perfect weeks from then on are worth 7.5 points (an additional 1/2 point for your 5th perfect week and above).
